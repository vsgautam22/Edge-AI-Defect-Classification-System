Outputs & Evidence – Context and Consent

This folder contains supporting evidence generated during the development
and evaluation of the Edge-AI Wafer Defect Classification System.

Contents include:
- Terminal output logs from model training, evaluation, auditing, and ONNX export
- Screenshots captured from the Visual Studio Code development environment
- Visual evidence of model performance metrics and deployment readiness

All screenshots and logs:
- Were generated by the project authors during local development
- Reflect genuine execution results of the implemented system
- Are provided strictly for documentation, evaluation, and review purposes

No proprietary, confidential, or third-party data is included.
All datasets referenced are either publicly sourced or manually curated
for academic and hackathon use, with no violation of usage rights.

These materials are intended to serve as transparent proof-of-work
and reproducibility evidence for reviewers and evaluators.
